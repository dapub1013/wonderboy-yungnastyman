## Act 1

[ ] Armour: [Luminous Armour](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Luminous_Armour)
  - `Underdark` In a locked and trapped opulent chest at Selûnite Outpost

[ ] Boots: [Boots of Aid and Comfort](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Boots_of_Aid_and_Comfort)
  - `Goblin Camp` Sold by Grat

[ ] Boots: [Boots of Stormy Clamour](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Boots_of_Stormy_Clamour)
  - `Underdark` Sold by Omeluum after completing his quest "Help Omeluum investigate the parasite" at Ebonlake Grotto

[ ] Boots: [Disintegrating Night Walkers](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Disintegrating_Night_Walkers)
  - `Grymforge` Worn by **Nere**; looted from his corpse `X: -854 Y: 780`

[ ] Gloves: [Gloves of Belligerent Skies](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Gloves_of_Belligerent_Skies)
  - `Crèche Y'llek` In an elegant chest in the Inquisitor's Chamber, along the southern wall of the room, on the western side

[ ] Gloves: [Hellrider's Pride](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Hellrider%27s_Pride)
  - `Druid Grove` Carried by **Zevlor** and can be either looted from him, bought, stolen, or earned as reward for the quests "Kill Kagha" or "Investigate Kagha"

[ ] Longsword: [Phalar Aluve](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Phalar_Aluve)
  - `Underdark` Embedded inside a rock

[ ] Quarterstave: [Staff of Arcane Blessing](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Staff_of_Arcane_Blessing)
  - `Underdark` Leaning against a table in the basement at Arcane Tower

[ ] Ring: [The Whispering Promise](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/The_Whispering_Promise)
  - `Goblin Camp` Sold by Grat, or by **Volo** at the Sacred Pool
